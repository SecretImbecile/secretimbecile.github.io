What is Game? What is Game is a game. It is an open-world freeroaming sandbox roleplaying adveture game platformer. It is infested with microtransactions and expensive DLC, cut by preorder bonus missions available at retail shops that only I know the location of. It has guns and Levelled Multiplayer and next-gen graphics and lies.

"What is game?" is a hobby game project in Unity3D.

The game follows the adventures of past-his-best videogame character, who in freak trans-dimensional accident discovers he is a fictional character starring in a documentary about videogames. Teaming up with the narrator of the documentary, he embarks on a new quest. To discover, in its purest essence, "What is Game?".
